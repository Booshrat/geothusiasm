Guess The Flag

    How to play

    1. Click on 'Start Game' button
    2. Insert your name and press 'Enter' on your keyboard
    3. A countdown for 3 seconds will start
    4. A flag will appear on screen with 4 answer buttons
    5. Click on the name of the country you believe the flag belongs to
    6. It will show if your answer is correct or not and will display an interesting fact about the country
    7. Click 'Next' button to go to the next round
    8. If you get 3 answers wrong, your game will be over
    9. Click 'Submit Score' button to store your score in the leaderboard
    10. Click 'Restart Game' button to restart your game
    11. You can view top 10 scores by first clicking on 'Home' button and then 'Leaderboard' button
