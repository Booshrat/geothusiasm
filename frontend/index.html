Guess The Flag

How to play

  1. Click on 'Start Game' button
  2. Insert your name and press enter or click 'Submit' button
  3. A countdown for 3 seconds will start
  4. A flag will appear on screen with 4 answer buttons
  5. Click on the name of the country you believe the flag belongs to
  6. It will show if your answer is correct or not and will display an interesting fact about the country
  7. Click 'Next' button to go to the next round
